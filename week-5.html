Custom 3D-Printed Phone Stand Project

Design Inspiration

My goal was to design a practical yet aesthetically pleasing phone stand. The stand should not only to provide stability and support for a smartphone but also to ensure ease of interaction, whether it be for video calling, media consumption, or as a desk accessory that complements the modern workspace.

Conceptualization and Modeling

Using CAD software, the phone stand was conceptualized with user ergonomics in mind. The design features a minimalist curve, catering to the form factor of contemporary smartphones while ensuring a robust base for stability. The process involved iterative modifications to achieve the perfect balance between form and function.

3D Printing Process - Equipment and Materials

• Printer: Original Prusa i3 MK3S (the print commenced with a heated bed to ensure optimal adhesion and a controlled print environment to mitigate warping)

• Material: PLA (given its low shrinkage properties)

• Layer Height: 0.2 mm

• Infill: 20%

Final Product

The completion of the 2-hour and 9-minute print culminated in a phone stand that was both functional and aesthetically pleasing!
