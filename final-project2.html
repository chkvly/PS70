Minimum Viable Product (MVP) Overview

Introduction

This document outlines the features and functionalities of the MVP for our project, highlighting the initial implementations and basic operations as demonstrated in the recent YouTube short.

MVP Features

  • Basic Functionality: The MVP showcases the core function of the project, focusing on demonstrating the basic operation without the enhancements found in the final product.
  • Simple User Interface: A straightforward and minimalistic user interface is used in the MVP to facilitate ease of use and understanding.
  • Key Operations: Essential operations of the project are highlighted, showing how they are initiated and their immediate outcomes.

Demonstration and Feedback

The MVP demonstration provides a clear view of the project's initial capabilities and gathers user feedback for future improvements.

For a direct look at the MVP in action, refer to the video here: YouTube MVP Demonstration.

Future Enhancements

Based on the feedback and observations from the MVP demo, several enhancements are planned to refine the functionality, user experience, and performance for the final product.
